# Learning and Growth

This area covers knowledge acquisition, skill development, and building systems for continuous learning. It includes reading, courses, knowledge management infrastructure, and deliberate practice.

The primary tension is between depth of learning and breadth of output — investing in learning compounds over time but competes with near-term creative production.
